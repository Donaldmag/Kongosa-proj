Features

Efficient media player with high quality sound

Kongossa app supports high quality and popular audio media file extensions to provide you with the most appreciated experience. Some of these extensions that are supported include MP3, AAC, FLAC, MQA, WAV, DSD,...etc

Upload your own media and discover new ones

Kongossa app bring together african artist and lets them spread the world with their music and culture. New artists are welcome on the plateform so as to have a place to start their music career and touch or interact with the public wordwide.

Visite artists page, like, comment and share links to your lovely ones

On Kongossa app you have the opportunity to visite your favorite artists page, get tuned with their albums and new released. We form a community through which you can like music tracks and comment derectly it. You can also share the links of tracks to lovely members on social media.

Connect to your Mobile, Desktop, Tablets, TV, Smartwatch,...

Kongossa app has no limit. You can connect to your Mobile, Desktop, Tablets, TV and Smartwatch in order to enjoy african music where ever you are.
